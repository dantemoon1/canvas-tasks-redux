# Privacy Policy

**Tasks for Canvas Redux**

Last updated: April 2026

## Overview

Tasks for Canvas Redux does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What data we access

The extension accesses assignment and course data directly from your Canvas instance via Canvas's own public API. This data never leaves your browser except to communicate with Canvas itself.

## What we store

The extension stores the following data locally in your browser:

- Your extension settings (theme preferences, view period, etc.)
- The list of Canvas domains you have explicitly enabled
- Any custom tasks you create within the sidebar

This data is stored using Chrome's `storage.sync` API and remains on your device.

## What we do NOT do

- We do not track your browsing history
- We do not send analytics or telemetry to any external server
- We do not collect personal identifiers
- We do not share data with third parties
- We do not use cookies

## Permissions

The extension requests website access only for Canvas domains you explicitly enable. No other websites are accessed.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
